About

The Basement War Gamers are proud to present Summer Slaughter 2023, our 6th annual Age of Sigmar gaming weekend. Event to take place at St. Gabe's Lodge located at 525 Jefferson St, Stowe, PA 19464. Space limited to 132 Age of Sigmar players in the GT, and 32 teams for the Doubles event. Event is first come, first served. Tickets are purchasable through Best Coast Pairings at the links below.

Events

Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament (Saturday and Sunday)

The Basement War Gamers club presents our 6th Annual Summer Slaughter Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament. This event will be 5 rounds (3 hours per round) of Matched Play games. Rules and Battleplans will come from the General's Handbook 2023. Army lists due by 11:59pm on Saturday, July 15th. Any players submitting an army list after this date will receive an automatic Yellow Card (see ITC Code of Conduct). All armies must be fully built, fully painted, and based. No exceptions. Scores will be submitted to the ITC for Hobby Track and standard ITC Competitive Scoring. Each Grand Tournament ticket includes lunch on Saturday and Sunday, as well as water, soda, and draft beer for those attendees that are 21 years of age or older with valid proof of age.

Should you have any questions please feel free to message us on Facebook or Twitter, or send us an email..
