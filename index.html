About

The Basement War Gamers are proud to present Summer Slaughter 2022, an Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament. Event to take place at St. Gabe's Lodge located at 525 Jefferson St, Stowe, PA 19464. Space limited to 100 Age of Sigmar players. Event is first come first served. Tickets are purchasable through Best Coast Pairings at the links below.

Each event ticket comes with hot buffet lunch on Saturday and cold buffet lunch on Sunday. Yuengling Lager draft beer and Coors Light draft beer also included in the ticket price for those attendees that are 21 years of age or older with valid proof of age. Water, soda, and mixed drinks extra at the bar areas.

Face Masks and/or Face Shields will be required to participate. A temperature check will take place each day for all attendees for the safety of all venue personnel, event staff, and participants.

Events

Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament

The Basement War Gamers club presents our 5th Annual Summer Slaughter Age of Sigmar Grant Tournament (ITC Major)

This event will be 5 rounds (3 hours per round) of Matched Play games. Rules and Battleplans will come from the most recent General's Handbook (2022).

Our initial player cap will be 100 players, but we may add space for more if the venue allows.

Ticket price is set at $100 per person. Refund cut-off date is 11:59pm on Sunday, June 19th. Ticket price includes a hot catered lunch on Saturday and cold catered lunch Sunday.

Army lists due by 11:59pm on Saturday, July 9th. Any players submitting an army list after this date will receive and automatic Yellow Card (see ITC Code of Conduct).

Battletomes, 2022 General's Handbook, and Points Changes released on or before Saturday, July 2nd will be allowed at the event.

FAQ's and Errata released on or before Saturday, July 9th will be used at the event.
